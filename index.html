How Our Email Checker Works

Advanced analysis for maximum deliverability and compliance

Our Email Compliance Checker analyzes your email content against multiple criteria to ensure maximum deliverability and legal compliance:

  • Spam Detection: The system scans your email content for characteristics commonly flagged by spam filters, including suspicious phrases, excessive capitalization, and misleading subject lines.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Automatically checks your email against GDPR, CAN-SPAM, CASL, and other regional regulations based on your target audience's location.
  • Industry-Specific Rules: Validates content against specialized guidelines for healthcare, finance, e-commerce, and other regulated industries.
  • Multi-Format Support: Processes plain text emails, HTML emails, and even images containing email text through OCR technology.
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Generates a detailed report highlighting compliance issues, spam risks, and recommendations for improvement.

2
Regulatory Check

3
Industry Validation

For regulated industries, the system checks for prohibited claims, required disclaimers, and special compliance requirements.

4
Report Generation

The tool compiles all findings into an easy-to-understand report with actionable recommendations and compliance scores for each category.

Website Uptime Monitor

24/7 monitoring to ensure your website is always available with our global tracking network

How Our Uptime Tracker Works

Continuous monitoring from multiple global locations

Our monitoring service constantly checks your website's availability and performance from multiple global locations:

  • Continuous Monitoring: Checks your website at configurable intervals (from 1 minute to 1 hour) from multiple global locations.
  • Instant Alerts: Notifies you immediately via email, SMS, or webhook when your site goes down or experiences performance degradation.
  • Performance Metrics: Tracks response times, downtime duration, and patterns to help identify underlying issues.
  • SSL Monitoring: Alerts you before your SSL certificate expires to prevent security warnings.
  • Historical Reporting: Provides detailed uptime statistics and trends over daily, weekly, and monthly periods.
  • Root Cause Analysis: When available, identifies common causes of downtime (DNS issues, server errors, etc.).

How It Works Behind the Scenes

1
Global Monitoring Network

Our distributed servers around the world periodically attempt to access your website using standard HTTP/HTTPS requests.

2
Response Validation

Each check verifies that the website returns a valid HTTP status code (200 OK) within the expected time frame.

3
Incident Detection

When multiple locations detect an issue, the system confirms the outage and triggers alerts.

4
Recovery Verification

The system continues checking more frequently until the site recovers, then sends a resolution notification.
